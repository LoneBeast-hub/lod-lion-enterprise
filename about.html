About Us

Our Brief History

Lion Edge Enterprises, incorporated in 2020, started construction actively since 2017 and has been renovating buildings from small to large, giving every building in their portfolio, a beautiful facelift that stands out among houses in its environment.

Having more than 5 years of experience in the construction industry, Lion Edge Enterprises understands clients dreams and expectations which is one of the goal that drives the team to deliver quality and superior services to their clients that exceed their expectations in terms of budget, quality and project delivery. We draw satisfaction from our commitment to work in partnership with our clients as satisfaction is our topmost priority, and at no extra or hidden charges.

Choose Lion Edge Enterprises for your next construction project and experience the highest level of quality and customer service.
